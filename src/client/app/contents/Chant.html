Chant. Meditate. Relax

"Ignore the problem, dwell on the divine, get relieved" is the motto behind such a novel thought. Almost, all possible problems that one could endure during their lifetime have a solution hosted in this app. You just pick your problem and the app will display mantras for you to chant over it with meditation, faith and honesty.

And for the religious Tamil mak-kal (Tamil People), as it is specially intended to be in Tamil language this app is a real blessing in disguise for you all. "May God bless you"
